Influences and Motivation

Diving Deeper

Falling in love with scuba diving and exploring underwater landscapes has led to a deeper personal concern for saving our world's oceans from athropocenic destruction and a passion for both enjoying the natural spaces on our planet before they are gone and doing what I can to impact the mitigation of harms done to these spaces. Pictured here is a baited shark dive off the coast of West Palm Beach FL USA, sharks pictured are Lemon Sharks.

Using Data Science to Impact Climate Change

Data Science is how we use data about the world around us to tell a story about the nature of our current reality as well as leverage cutting age machine learning techniques to predict future outcomes both real and imagined. It is my hope to leverage Machine Learning Engineering in the corporate space to help leaders of industry to not only become compliant in minimizing their ecological footprint but to exceed expections and inspire other corporations to do the same.
